The NetherLauncher Story

NetherLauncher was created to make installing modpacks and modloaders such as Forge and LiteLoader, as well as mods, very easy.

NetherLauncher is in development, so I may not provide specific help. To submit a bug click here (GitHub).

Mods Overview

I use Forge for my mods, but in the future I plan to do some LiteLoader mods as well.

  • Bone'n'Blaze: Adds extra tools made from bone and blaze items to create tools that can cook / smelt the harvests.
  • SocialBlocks: Integration between Minecraft / Screenshots, with platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, and others to be added. Click here to contribute!
