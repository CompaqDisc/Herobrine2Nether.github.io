YouTube may be life, but not without WiFi.

YouTube

You can see the early life of YouTube in 2005 (months after creation).

YouTube was founded in 2005 by Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim, employees of PayPal at the time. Over 2006, YouTube was the fastest growing website on the internet. In 2006 it was announced that Google would buy the company for $1.65 billion in stock, which was, at the time Google's second largest acquisition. YouTube also wins Time magazine's Person of the Year award in 2006. It was estimated that in 2007, YouTube used as much bandwidth as the entire internet in 2000. Now that's a lot of downloading! After a redesign in 2010, it was estimated that YouTube was serving over 2 billion videos a day.

American Idol

Created in 2002 by Simon Fuller, aired on Fox. It soon became, and still is one of the most successful TV shows in American history. The success of the show likely "saved fox" according to Simon. American Idol became the most watched non-scripted primetime television series for nearly a decade.
