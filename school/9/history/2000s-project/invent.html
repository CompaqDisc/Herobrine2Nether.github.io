Windows 2000 and XP

Windows 2000 was the first of Microsoft operating systems to include many of the things we later see in XP, Vista, and 7. Things like easier file management and recovery, as well as accessibility features. XP on the other hand, (codename Whistler, released 2001, support ended April 2014) came with a much simpler graphical style, WiFi support, bluetooth capability, and more.
